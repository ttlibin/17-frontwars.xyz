FREQUENTLY TRANSMITTED QUERIES

Common tactical questions and strategic answers

How do I report a bug in FrontWars?

Use the contact form above and select "SYSTEM MALFUNCTION" as your mission classification. Provide detailed steps to reproduce the issue and include any error messages.

Can I suggest new features for FrontWars?

Absolutely! Select "STRATEGIC ENHANCEMENT" in the contact form and describe your proposed feature in detail. Our development team reviews all suggestions.

How do I get help with gameplay?

Choose "TACTICAL SUPPORT" in the contact form and describe the specific aspect of gameplay you need assistance with. Our experienced players can provide guidance.

Is there a FrontWars community forum?

Yes! Join our community discussions on Discord and Reddit. Links are available in our community section on the main page.

How can I become a FrontWars partner?

Select "ALLIANCE PROPOSAL" in the contact form and describe your partnership proposal. We're always interested in strategic collaborations.

What's the best way to stay updated on FrontWars?

Subscribe to our newsletter using the contact form, follow us on social media, and join our Discord community for real-time updates.
